2. Speaking structure

Start: I would like to talk about wild animals.
Main part: Name examples, describe one animal, explain where it lives and why it is interesting.
Opinion: Say what you think and why wild animals should be protected.

Useful phrases

One example of a wild animal is ...
It lives in ...
It usually eats ...
In my opinion, wild animals are important because ...
We should protect them because ...

Questions

Which wild animal do you find interesting? Why?
Where do wild animals live?
What dangers do wild animals face?
Should people visit zoos? Give a reason.

Sample answer

I would like to talk about wild animals. One example is the lion. Lions live in the savannah and they usually eat meat. I think lions are very interesting because they are strong and live in groups. Many wild animals are in danger because people destroy their habitats. In my opinion, we should protect wild animals because they are important for nature.

2. Speaking structure

Start: I would like to talk about winter.
Main part: Talk about weather, clothes and typical winter activities.
Opinion: Say whether you like winter and explain your reason.

Useful phrases

In winter, the weather is often ...
People usually wear ...
You can ... in winter.
One problem in winter is ...
I like / don't like winter because ...

Questions

What is the weather like in winter?
What do people wear in winter?
Which winter activity do you like?
What can be difficult or dangerous in winter?

Sample answer

I would like to talk about winter. In winter, the weather is often cold and snowy. People wear warm jackets, hats, scarves and gloves. Many people like skiing, ice skating or building a snowman. But winter can also be dangerous because roads can be icy. I like winter because snow looks beautiful, but sometimes it is too cold for me.

2. Speaking structure

Start: I would like to talk about my free time.
Main part: Talk about hobbies, where you do them, who you do them with and how often.
Opinion: Explain why free time is important for you.

Useful phrases

In my free time, I like to ...
I usually do this with ...
I often go to ...
I do this once a week / every day / at the weekend.
Free time is important because ...

Questions

What do you like doing in your free time?
Who do you spend your free time with?
How often do you do your hobby?
Is free time important for students? Why?

Sample answer

I would like to talk about my free time. In my free time, I like playing football and listening to music. I usually play football with my friends at the park or in a sports club. I do this twice a week. Free time is important because students can relax and have fun. In my opinion, hobbies are also good because you can learn new skills.
