2. Speaking structure

Start: I would like to talk about school.
Main part: Talk about your subjects, your school day, teachers, classmates and school rules.
Opinion: Say what you like or do not like about school and give a reason.

Useful phrases

My favourite subject is ...
My school day usually starts at ...
At school, we have to ...
I like school because ...
In my opinion, school is important because ...

Questions

What is your favourite subject? Why?
What is a normal school day like for you?
Which school rules are important?
What do you like or dislike about school?

Sample answer

I would like to talk about school. My school day usually starts in the morning. My favourite subject is English because I like speaking and learning new words. At school, I meet my classmates and teachers. We have to do homework and follow school rules. In my opinion, school is important because we learn many things for our future. Sometimes school is stressful, but it can also be interesting.

2. Speaking structure

Start: I would like to talk about sport.
Main part: Talk about different sports, where people do them, what equipment they need and why sport is healthy.
Opinion: Say which sport you like or dislike and explain your reason.

Useful phrases

One popular sport is ...
You can play / do it in ...
For this sport, you need ...
Sport is good for your body because ...
My favourite sport is ... because ...

Questions

Which sport do you like best? Why?
Do you prefer team sports or individual sports?
Why is sport good for your health?
Should students do more sport at school? Give a reason.

Sample answer

I would like to talk about sport. One popular sport is football. You can play it on a football field, and you need a ball, sports shoes and a team. Sport is good for your body because it keeps you fit and healthy. It can also be fun because you meet other people. My favourite sport is basketball because it is fast and exciting. In my opinion, everyone should try to do some sport.

2. Speaking structure

Start: I would like to talk about Australia.
Main part: Talk about the country, famous cities, animals, sights and the weather.
Opinion: Say whether you would like to visit Australia and explain why.

Useful phrases

Australia is a country and a continent.
A famous city in Australia is ...
Australia is known for ...
You can see animals like ...
I would / would not like to visit Australia because ...

Questions

What do you know about Australia?
Which animals live in Australia?
Which famous sights can tourists visit?
Would you like to travel to Australia? Why or why not?

Sample answer

I would like to talk about Australia. Australia is a country and a continent. Famous cities are Sydney, Melbourne and Canberra. Australia is known for kangaroos, koalas, beautiful beaches and the Great Barrier Reef. The weather can be very hot and dry in some parts of the country. I would like to visit Australia because I think the animals and landscapes are very interesting.
