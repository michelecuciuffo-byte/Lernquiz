Mini task

  • Choose one school picture.
  • Describe the picture in 4–6 sentences.
  • Say what the students are doing.
  • Describe the place and the atmosphere.
  • Say if you would like to be there and why.
Possible questions
What can you see in the picture?
What are the students doing?
What can you see in the classroom or playground?
What subject could it be?
Does the atmosphere look calm or lively?
Which school activities do you like best?
Example start:
In this picture I can see students at school. Some of them are working together and they seem focused. The classroom looks busy and interesting. I think the lesson could be enjoyable because the students are active and involved.

Mini task

  • Choose one winter picture.
  • Describe what the people are doing.
  • Say what the weather is like.
  • Name the clothes or objects you can see.
  • Say if you would enjoy this activity.
Possible questions
What is the weather like in winter?
What can people do in this picture?
Which winter activity looks the most fun?
What do people need for this activity?
Is the activity easy or difficult?
Do you prefer winter activities or summer activities?
Example start:
In this picture I can see people enjoying winter. They are wearing warm clothes because it is very cold. The ground is covered with snow or ice, and the activity looks exciting. I think winter can be fun when you do something active outside.

Mini task

  • Choose one free-time picture.
  • Describe what the people are doing.
  • Say if the activity is active or relaxing.
  • Say why this hobby could be fun.
  • Say if you would like to do it yourself.
Possible questions
What do you usually do in your free time?
Do you prefer active or quiet hobbies?
Which free-time activity in the pictures looks the most interesting?
Why is free time important?
Which hobby would you recommend to a friend?
Example start:
In this picture I can see people spending their free time together. They look relaxed and happy, and the activity seems enjoyable. In my opinion, free-time activities are important because they help people relax and have fun with others.

4. Useful speaking phrases

These sentence starters can help you speak more fluently in the exam.

Describing a picture

English Deutsch
In this picture I can see ...Auf diesem Bild kann ich ... sehen.
In the foreground there is / there are ...Im Vordergrund ist / sind ...
In the background I can see ...Im Hintergrund kann ich ... sehen.
The person / people look(s) ...Die Person / Die Leute sehen ... aus.
The weather seems to be ...Das Wetter scheint ... zu sein.

English Deutsch

I would like to ...Ich würde gerne ...
